Farmers Market Associate

Fire Swamp Provisions is seeking a friendly and enthusiastic Farmers Market Associate to join our team at the Marin Farmers Markets on Thursdays and Sundays.

  • Pay$23-25/hr, plus tips
  • ScheduleThursdays & Sundays, 5:30 AM to ~2 PM
  • LocationMarin Farmers Markets

Organization & location

Fire Swamp Provisions. Marin Farmers Markets (Thursdays and Sundays).

Job Overview

Fire Swamp Provisions is seeking a friendly and enthusiastic Farmers Market Associate to join our team at the Marin Farmers Markets on Thursdays and Sundays. The successful candidate will load our van at the bakery, set up the stand to our standards, proactively engage customers, and maintain strong communication with ownership on market trends.

Responsibilities

  • Set up and work the booth in a timely and organized fashion.
  • Welcome and assist customers, sharing information about our products and farmers.
  • Keep the booth organized & stocked at all times; breakdown at the end of markets.
  • Communicate with ownership in a timely manner.
  • Promote our offerings and educate visitors on the quality and origin of our products.
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of our market space and display.
  • Process sales and payments accurately.
  • Enforce market policies and safety guidelines as needed.
  • Assist with additional promotional activities and signage as needed.
  • Represent Fire Swamp Provisions with professionalism and enthusiasm.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Qualifications

  • Strong customer service and communication skills.
  • Ability to work outdoors in various weather conditions.
  • Previous experience in retail, food service, or farmers markets is a plus.
  • Passion for local food, farming, and community engagement.
  • Reliable, punctual, and adaptable to market needs.

Schedule

  • Thursdays and Sundays during market hours (5:30AM to approximately 2PM).
  • Additional days if available.

Benefits

  • Opportunity to work with local farmers and community members.
  • Gain experience in retail, food sales, and event setup.
  • Friendly team environment with a focus on community and quality.

Compensation

  • $23-25/hour, plus tips
  • $30/market trade allowance
  • 25% discount on all consumables

AM Baker, PM Baker

  • AM Baker: 40 hours/week Monday through Friday, start time between 3-5 AM.
  • PM Baker: 30-40 hours/week Wednesday through Saturday (4 ten hour shifts).

Salary / rate

Starting $23-$26/hour depending on experience, attitude, and references. Negotiable after a 60 day trial period.

Professional Requirements

ServeSafe or equivalent certification (can be certified post-hire), minimum one year bakery experience or two years culinary experience.

About us

Fire Swamp Provisions is a very small artisan bakery focusing on local, market driven breads and pastries made by hand with love and care.

About the role

We are looking for two hard working, smart, self-motivated, and enthusiastic bakers to fill out our small and excellent team.

AM Baker will work primarily with the head baker to handle a variety of production, including wholesale and retail breads, laminated pastries, scones, pie, and more.

PM Baker will work primarily with the owner to bake, finish, and pack items for markets, plus limited production.

Responsibilities

Scaling, mixing, dividing and shaping of various naturally leavened and yeasted doughs. Includes baking, packing, and labelling, as well. Other duties needed include cleaning, organizing, rotation of product, inventory and facilities maintenance, and any other duties as assigned.

Candidate requirements

  • Minimum 1-2 years experience in a production bakery (negotiable for the right candidate)
  • Common sense, exceptional attitude, and willingness to check your ego at the door.
  • Understanding of baker's math and percentages, dough fermentation and lamination
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Able to lift 50+ pounds frequently
  • Able to stand on one's feet throughout an entire shift
  • Able to work clean, fast, and with a sense of purpose

Please provide 3 professional references with both phone numbers and email addresses. Email your resume to fireswampprovisions@gmail.com.
