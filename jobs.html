Farmers Market Associate

Be the friendly face of Fire Swamp at the Marin farmers markets. Set up the booth, talk bread and pastries, and take great care of our regulars.

  • Pay$23-25/hr + tips
  • ScheduleThu & Sun, 5:30 AM to ~2 PM
  • LocationMarin markets, San Rafael

Overview

We're looking for a friendly and enthusiastic Farmers Market Associate to join our team at the Marin farmers markets. You'll be the face of Fire Swamp Provisions, sharing what we make and taking great care of the people who come back every week.

What you'll do

  • Set up and operate the booth in an organized fashion
  • Engage customers and share product information
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of our market space
  • Process sales accurately
  • Represent the company professionally

What we're looking for

  • Strong customer service skills
  • Ability to work outdoors in various weather conditions
  • Prior retail, food service, or farmers market experience preferred
  • A passion for local food, farming, and community engagement

Schedule

Thursdays and Sundays during market hours (5:30 AM to approximately 2 PM).

Compensation

$23-25/hour, plus tips. A $30/market trade allowance, and a 25% discount on all consumables.

How to apply: Email Alex at fireswampprovisions@gmail.com with a quick note about yourself and any relevant experience.

AM Baker

Early mornings on the bench, mixing, shaping, and baking the day's breads and pastries from scratch.

  • Pay$23-26/hr
  • Schedule40 hrs/wk, Mon-Fri, 3-5 AM start
  • LocationSan Rafael, CA 94901

Overview

Fire Swamp Provisions is a very small artisan bakery focusing on local, market-driven breads and pastries made by hand with love and care. We're hiring an AM Baker to lead early production and get the day's bake out the door.

What you'll do

  • Dough production: mixing, shaping, and baking
  • Cleaning, organizing, and rotation of product
  • Inventory and facilities maintenance
  • Packing and labeling items

Qualifications

  • Minimum 1-2 years experience in a production bakery
  • Understanding of baker's math and fermentation
  • Ability to lift 50+ pounds frequently
  • ServSafe or equivalent certification

Schedule

40 hours/week, Monday through Friday. Start time between 3 and 5 AM.

Compensation

$23-26/hour depending on experience, attitude, and references.

How to apply: Email your resume and three professional references to fireswampprovisions@gmail.com.
